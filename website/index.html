A floating desktop player for Windows. Play local playlists, download from YouTube, Spotify, TikTok, and more, all from a minimal widget on your screen.

Features

  1. Floating widget
    Stays on top of any app.
  2. Folder playlists
    Your music, organized on disk.
  3. Download from web
    YouTube, Spotify, TikTok, and more.
  4. Hide & show
    Alt+\ or the top chevron.

How it works

  1. Download
    Install Beats on Windows in one click.
  2. Add your music
    Use a folder or paste a link.
  3. Place it anywhere
    Drag the album art to move it.
  4. Hide when needed
    Alt+\ or the top chevron.

Windows app

Your music, always within reach.

Beats is a floating desktop player for Windows. Play local playlists, download tracks from YouTube, Spotify, TikTok, and more — all from a minimal pill on your screen.

Download View on GitHub

Windows 10 / 11 · 64-bit · Published by Delexo

Features

  • Floating pill — compact controls that stay on top of other apps.
  • Folder playlists — your library stays portable on disk.
  • Download from web — paste a link; MP3s save into your playlist.
  • Hide & show — slide off-screen with Ctrl+\ or the top chevron.

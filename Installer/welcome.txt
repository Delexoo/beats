Welcome to Beats

This installer will set up Beats on your PC — a floating desktop music player for Windows 10 and 11.

What you get:
  • A minimal always-on-top widget for playback controls
  • Folder-based playlists under My Music\Beats
  • Download tracks from YouTube, Spotify, TikTok, and more

No account is required. Beats runs locally on your machine.

Need help after installing? Visit:
  https://delexoo.github.io/beats/help.html

Click Next to choose an install location, or Cancel to exit.

Welcome to Beats

This installer will set up Beats on your PC — a floating desktop music player
for Windows 10 and 11.

What you get:
  • A minimal always-on-top widget for playback controls
  • Folder-based playlists under My Music\Beats
  • Optional download from links you paste (YouTube, Spotify, and more)

No account is required. Beats runs locally on your machine.

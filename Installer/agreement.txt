PART B — PRIVACY POLICY
================================================================================

1. SUMMARY

Beats is a local-first application. It runs on your PC and keeps your music
on your disk. We do not operate a Beats account system, cloud library, or
analytics service tied to your identity.


2. INFORMATION STORED ON YOUR DEVICE

Beats may store the following on your computer:

  • Application settings in %APPDATA%\Beats\settings.json
  • Liked songs and saved collections as local JSON files
  • Playlist folders under your Music directory
  • Cached album artwork in %APPDATA%\Beats\artwork\
  • Download tools in %APPDATA%\Beats\tools\
  • Optional crash logs in %APPDATA%\Beats\

We do not automatically upload this data to Delexo servers.


3. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not require your name, email, phone number, or payment information.
The installer does not send us personal data when you download or install
Beats.


4. NETWORK ACTIVITY

Beats may connect to the internet when you download audio, fetch artwork,
update third-party tools, or open help links. Those requests go from your PC
to the relevant services. We do not receive the contents of your downloads.


5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Beats is not affiliated with YouTube, Spotify, TikTok, SoundCloud, or any
other platform. Third-party tools may process data according to their own
policies.


6. DATA DELETION

You can delete Beats data at any time from %APPDATA%\Beats\ and your music
folders. Uninstalling Beats does not automatically delete your music.
